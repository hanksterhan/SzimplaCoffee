## Notes on Seeding Strategy

1. **Phase 1 (Tier A)**: Add the 10 Tier A roasters first. Crawl and verify manually. These are the "known good" starting set.
2. **Phase 2 (Tier B verified)**: Add Tier B merchants, run a crawl, check metadata quality scores. Promote to A after verified order.
3. **Phase 3 (Tier C)**: Add Tier C only after Phase 1/2 are stable. Use for discovery coverage.

## Platform Detection Priority

For all merchants, run `szimpla merchant add <url>` which will auto-detect Shopify vs WooCommerce vs custom.

Most roasters listed here are Shopify. WooCommerce is noted where confirmed.

## Caveats

- URLs may have changed since this list was compiled — verify each before adding
- Some roasters may have closed or changed ownership
- Subscription vs one-time purchase availability varies by merchant
- Freshness signals (roast dates, roast-to-order) must be verified during crawl
