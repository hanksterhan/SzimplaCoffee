## Why the ticket failed
The ticket acceptance criterion required origin fill rate ≥70% across all 890 products. That target was not reached.

Investigation showed the remaining gap is largely structural, not parser-only:
1. Many rows in `products` are not real single-origin coffees (accessories, filters, shirts, subscriptions, bundles, gift items, blends, cold brew SKUs), but all are currently categorized as `product_category='coffee'`.
2. `backfill-metadata` only has access to `product.name` because product descriptions are not stored on the `products` table; crawler-time parsing can see descriptions, but backfill cannot recover that context later.
3. Filtering out obvious non-coffee merch-like SKUs showed origin fill is already ~70% for likely real coffee products (538/773).
4. 18 products had `origin_text` but no `origin_country`; the implemented fix recovered 15 of them via region→country mapping.
